What is Pinnacle

Pinnacle invites 4 winners from each of the world’s largest 50 collegiate hackathons to compete in an epic hack-off in late 2021.

Our participants, who represent the brightest developers, designers, and creators in the nation, will receive invitations throughout the Pinnacle 2020-2021 hackathon season. If you'd like to earn a ticket to Pinnacle, win one of the qualifying events listed below, and stay tuned for more updates on our website.
