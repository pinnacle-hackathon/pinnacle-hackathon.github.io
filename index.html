The Olympics of Hackathons

What is Pinnacle?

Pinnacle is a competitive collegiate hackathon where 200 of the world's top hackers get together in San Francisco for 36 hours. Unlike a traditional hackathon, Pinnacle invites 4 winners from each of the world's top 50 collegiate hackathons to compete in an epic tournament-styled hackathon.

Our participants, who represent the brightest developers, designers, and creators in the nation, will be flown out to San Francisco for a weekend at Pinnacle in late 2020. Want to participate? Stay tuned for more updates on our website.
